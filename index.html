About

I Arsh Kaur, I am an international selling acrylic artist from Birmingham, England.
Having done sketches with pen and pencil ever since I could remember, I First picked up a paintbrush in 2015 and have been teaching myself this challenging yet rewarding form of self-expression ever since.
Primarily, I work with acrylic paint and occasionally use watercolour paint or gouache. I enjoy painting landscapes and capturing nature onto canvas or acrylic and watercolour paper; with each work, I try to evoke a sense of calm and serenity.
I make custom pieces and enjoy collaborating to make artwork whether its on canvas paper or in the form of a giclee fine art print.
